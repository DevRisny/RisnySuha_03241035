Quick Tips

Here are a few simple tips to keep your flowers fresh and beautiful:

  • Trim stems at an angle before placing in water.
  • Use clean, fresh water and change it daily.
  • Keep flowers away from direct sunlight and heat.
  • Remove wilted petals to maintain freshness.
  • Add flower food or a drop of sugar for longer life.
